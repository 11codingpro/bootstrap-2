Bootstrap container example!

The "container-fluid" class in Bootstrap 5 creates a container that spans the entire width of the viewport, adjusting to different screen sizes without margins or gutters. It's ideal for full-width elements like headers, footers, or hero sections, providing a seamless transition across devices
